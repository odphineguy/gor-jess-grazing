Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 7 days in advance for standard packages and 14 days for luxury or custom orders. However, we do accept rush orders when availability allows. Contact us directly for last-minute requests.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Los Angeles, Inland Empire, and Orange County. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order so we can create a customized selection that everyone can enjoy.

Do you provide setup service?

Yes! Setup and styling is included with our Premium and Luxury packages. For Starter packages, setup can be added for an additional fee. Our team will arrive early to arrange everything beautifully before your guests arrive.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available for cancellations made 7+ days before your event. Cancellations made 3-7 days in advance receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable but can be rescheduled for a future date.

How long do the grazing tables last at an event?

Our items are fresh and best enjoyed within 4-6 hours of setup. For all-day events, we recommend choosing items that hold up well at room temperature or scheduling a refresh visit. We'll advise on the best options for your event duration.
