Our Story

Artisanal Grazing Tables & Charcuterie Creations

Founded in the heart of Los Angeles, Gor-Jess Grazzing brings together a passion for fine foods and artistic presentation. Our founder, Jessica, combines her culinary expertise with an eye for design to create show-stopping grazing experiences.

We source only the highest quality local and imported ingredients, from artisan cheeses and cured meats to seasonal fruits and house-made accompaniments. Each arrangement is carefully curated to delight both the eyes and the palate.

Whether you're hosting an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, our grazing tables become the centerpiece of your event, encouraging connection and conversation over beautiful, delicious food.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Los Angeles, Inland Empire, and Orange County. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for most events, especially during peak seasons (spring and summer). For weddings and large events, 4-6 weeks notice is ideal.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can create grazing tables with vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, or dairy-free options. Please let us know about any dietary needs when placing your order.

How long does setup take?

Setup typically takes 1-2 hours depending on the size of your grazing table and the complexity of the design. We'll arrive well before your event start time to ensure everything is perfect.

What's included in the price?

Our pricing includes all food, styling, setup, and rental of any serving boards or stands we provide. It does not include staffing unless specifically arranged.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes! We offer tasting sessions for wedding and large event clients. Tastings are scheduled by appointment and include a sampling of our most popular items.
